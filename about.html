About Felicia M. Boyd

Felicia Boyd, a proud native of Victoria, Texas, has dedicated over two decades to shaping young minds through the creative arts. A graduate of Texas A&M Kingsville with a focus on Theatre Design and Art, Felicia has passionately taught Theatre and Art across South and Central Texas for 24 years.
